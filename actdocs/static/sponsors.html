The Amazon Development Center Germany GmbH develops innovative technologies for Amazon Web Services (AWS). In 2006, AWS began offering IT infrastructure services to businesses in the form of web services – now commonly known as cloud computing. One of the key benefits of cloud computing is the opportunity to replace up-front capital infrastructure expenses with low variable costs that scale as a business grows. With the Cloud, businesses no longer need to plan for and procure servers and other IT infrastructure weeks or months in advance. Instead, they can instantly spin up hundreds or thousands of servers in minutes and deliver results faster. The AWS platform has grown rapidly since the launch of the first service and it is now the underlying infrastructure for businesses around the world from start-ups to enterprises to government agencies.

neo4j


Neo4j is an open-source NoSQL database that is specialized in storing, managing and querying data relationships. As everything around us is highly connected and variably structured information, a database built for that purpose allows you to gain new insights in your existing data and combine many datasources into dedicated views of your data. Neo4j runs on all platforms, drivers are available in most languages and its expressive query language and modern web-based UI makes it easy to get started. Neo4j is used by many large corporations, startups, individuals and open source projects to answer questions they couldn't solve before and provide new competitive advantages.
